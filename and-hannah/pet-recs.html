Pet's Local Favorites

The spots Pet grew up visiting or where he finds the most "soul" in Chiang Mai.

Khao Soi Lung Prakit

The legendary "Netflix" Khao Soi. Rich, creamy curry noodles. Order the beef version and ask for extra pickles.

North Gate Jazz Co-op

Grab a beer and stand on the sidewalk. This is where the local musicians hang out. Best vibes after 9 PM.
