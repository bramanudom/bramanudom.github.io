Our Favorite Spots

Toggle between our picks to see how we each experience Chiang Mai.

Khao Soi Lung Prakit

The legendary "Netflix" Khao Soi. Rich, creamy curry noodles. Order the beef version and ask for extra pickles.

North Gate Jazz Co-op

Grab a beer and stand on the sidewalk. This is where the local musicians hang out. Best vibes after 9 PM.

Bua Tong Waterfalls

Limestone waterfalls you can actually climb vertically like a lizard. It's a surreal and refreshing jungle experience.

Graph Cafe (One Nimman)

Where coffee meets art. Their charcoal lattes and minimal aesthetic are a must-see for design lovers.
