Travel FAQ

What is the dress code?

Tropical Formal. For men, light suits or linen. For women, midi or maxi dresses in jewel tones. We encourage you to wear white if you like—we won't be!

How do I get around Chiang Mai?

Download the **Grab** app (like Uber). It is very affordable and reliable. For a more local experience, hop in a Red Truck (Songthaew) for about 30 THB per person within the city.

Weather in November?

November is the start of the "cool season." Days are sunny (around 30°C) and evenings are beautiful and crisp (around 18°C).
